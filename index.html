DRAFT

Overview: Standard, Live Offline Draft will begin on August 27th, 2PM PST (11AM HST). Each team owner will have 120 seconds to make a selection. Please enter the Live Draft Lobby at least 30 minutes before the draft starts.

Keepers: Team owners from last season: You are entitled to 1 keeper. You may choose to forgo your keeper selection and be entered into the randomized "Keeper Round". If you do not set a keeper by August 27th, 12PM PST (9AM HST), you will be automatically entered into the "Keeper Round".

Keeper Round: Will be conducted when the draft starts on August 27th, 2PM PST (11AM HST). For new team owners: You will automatically be entered in the "Keeper Round".

Draft Order: The snake draft order will be randomized 30 minutes prior to the draft.

Skype Call: The video chatroom will begin approximately 1PM PST (10AM HST).

Average Draft Positions (ADP): NFL.COM Fantasy, ESPN/CBS/Yahoo!

In Tep We Trust

Tepception started in the 2013 NFL season when a group of engineering students found a way to take their love of football and competitiveness to another level. The league's name was inspired by University of Hawaii College of Engineering professor Dr. Thaddeus "Tep" Dobry. For decades, Tep guided aspiring engineers to accomplish academic goals and pursue their passions. Christian Damo once bummed him for a cigarette.
